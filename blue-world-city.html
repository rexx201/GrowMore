Blue World City

Blue World City Islamabad stands as a prominent and distinguished residential society within the city, offering an elite standard of living. This vast project is a collaboration between the Blue Group of Companies (BGC) and the Shan Jian Municipal Engineering Company. Situated in close proximity to the Chakri Interchange and the recently developed Rawalpindi Ring Road, the housing society enjoys a strategically advantageous location.
Blue World City Islamabad has gained significant popularity among investors, primarily due to its affordability compared to other housing ventures in the area. It has emerged as a highly favorable choice for individuals seeking suitable properties or residential plots for investment purposes.
Additionally, the project offers convenient online services, including Blue World City confirmation, online installment payments, certificate of registration verification, and application status tracking. The society is divided into several distinct sectors, such as General Square, Overseas Square, Blue Hills Country Farms, Blue World Economic Zone, Awami Villas, Orbital Flats, and the budget-friendly Awami Residential Complex. Each sector caters to different preferences and requirements, providing a diverse range of options for potential residents and investors alike.

Blue Group Of Companies

With a remarkable presence of 18 years in Pakistan, the Blue Group of Companies (BGC) has established itself as a leading provider of exceptional real estate development services. Spearheaded by Saad Nazir, the son of ex-Deputy Commissioner Lahore, Chaudhry Nazir, the company boasts a rich legacy and expertise in the field.
The success of the Blue Group of Companies can be attributed to its dedicated team of over 300 individuals, each contributing their unique capacities and skills. This dynamic workforce has propelled the company to become one of the most diversified conglomerates in Pakistan, reflecting its commitment to continuous growth and progress.
The developers are widely recognized for their emphasis on enhancement and innovation, constantly striving to exceed expectations and deliver superior results. Since its inception in 1998 in Lahore, Pakistan, BGC has been providing architectural design and construction services to valued clients, quickly capturing a substantial market share through its exceptional offerings.

Blue World City Owners & Developers

Established in 1998, Blue World City is a prestigious project owned by the renowned Blue Group of Companies (BGC). The sole proprietor of this esteemed company is Saad Nazeer, who happens to be the son of Ch. Nazeer, the former Deputy Commissioner of Lahore. BGC has garnered a well-deserved reputation as a highly reputable real estate developer, earning the trust and admiration of clients over the years.
BGC takes pride in its portfolio of successful developments, including notable projects like PIA Cooperative Housing Society, Center Park Lahore, and Blue Town Sapphire. These accomplishments have solidified BGC's position as a leader in the real estate industry, known for their excellence in delivering exceptional projects.
Adding to the significance of Blue World City's history, the project is developed in collaboration with the renowned Shah Jian Municipal Engineering Company. This collaboration has brought forth remarkable news that resonates strongly in Pakistan's real estate market.
Overall, Blue World City, under the ownership of BGC and with the partnership of Shah Jian Municipal Engineering Company, stands as a remarkable and noteworthy venture in the realm of real estate development in Pakistan.

Blue World City NOC

Blue World City is a prestigious housing society that holds the distinction of being legally approved with a valid NOC from the district council. It offers a plethora of investment opportunities at reasonable rates, making it an attractive option for individuals seeking profitable ventures. The Waterfront District Block within Blue World City adds another dimension to the project, providing clients with a highly sought-after and legally sanctioned opportunity to yield profitable results in the twin cities of Islamabad and Rawalpindi. It serves as the perfect complement to the overall offerings of Blue World City, catering to the needs and aspirations of investors who value legality and the potential for financial success.

Blue World City Location

When considering an investment in real estate, the location holds paramount importance for investors. The location not only influences the sense of security but also determines the accessibility to essential amenities for residents. Therefore, investors must carefully evaluate the advantages and disadvantages of a property's location before finalizing their investment decision.
Blue World City Islamabad, situated on the main Chakri Road, offers a highly favorable location for prospective investors. The project enjoys close proximity to the Chakri Interchange, providing convenient access to the M-2 Lahore-Islamabad Motorway. Additionally, the project is located relatively near the new Islamabad International Airport, further enhancing its accessibility and potential convenience for residents and investors alike. The strategic placement of Blue World City Islamabad ensures ease of travel and connectivity to major transportation hubs, making it an enticing option for individuals seeking a well-located investment opportunity.

Blue World City Master Plan

Blue World City in Rawalpindi is strategically designed to evolve into a thriving metropolitan hub, bringing forth significant economic progress and an enhanced lifestyle for its residents. The project incorporates innovative features and efficient management practices throughout its development, aiming to deliver smarter outcomes. The implementation of advanced infrastructure and thoughtful urban planning contributes to the creation of a superior living environment, offering a unique blend of modernity and aesthetic appeal.
The vision behind Blue World City Rawalpindi originated from the property owners' aspiration to establish a residential society that provides affordable and well-designed living spaces. In addition to catering to the residential needs, the project also aims to create an innovative and consumer-centric commercial zone, promoting a dynamic and productive work environment. The holistic approach to development ensures that Blue World City Rawalpindi is not just a residential project but a comprehensive community that addresses the diverse needs and aspirations of its residents and businesses alike.
The developer's extensive allocation of 5000 Kanal of land adds immense value and desirability to the project. The land is thoughtfully divided into various blocks, offering a diverse range of options for potential buyers. Blue World City presents a wide selection of private and commercial plots, as well as farmhouses for sale, catering to different preferences and requirements.
The project encompasses several well-defined blocks, each with its unique features and offerings. These include the General Square, Overseas Square, Blue Hills Country Farms, Awami Villas, Blue World Economic Zone, Orbital Flats, and the low-cost Awami Residential Complex. Each block is designed to cater to specific needs and preferences, providing residents and investors with a variety of choices to suit their individual preferences.
Detailed information about each block and its distinctive characteristics can be obtained, providing potential buyers with a comprehensive understanding of the diverse options available within the project. This meticulous planning ensures that Blue World City offers a range of options to accommodate different lifestyles and investment objectives.
