Pine City Islamabad Introduction

Pine Hills, an awe-inspiring development project brought to you by Pine City Developer, is nestled amidst the picturesque Makhnial Hills, a mere 30 minutes' drive from Islamabad. It is conveniently located just 3 kilometers away from Highland Country Club, 12 kilometers from Monal Restaurant, and 21 kilometers from Islamabad.
Spanning over one million square feet, Pine Hills offers a harmonious fusion of tranquility and recreational delights. The meticulously designed indoor and outdoor amenities are thoughtfully curated to rejuvenate your senses and provide a truly immersive experience. Whether it's the serene surroundings, the captivating views, or the range of recreational facilities, Pine Hills promises to create an enchanting atmosphere that leaves a lasting impression.

Pine City Islamabad Developers:

Encompassing the essence of tranquility and embracing the epitome of luxury, Pine City is an expansive project that seeks to harmonize nature with a contemporary sensibility. It represents not just a mere development endeavor but a visionary concept envisioned by the esteemed Pine City Developer, Major (R) Siddique. His childhood explorations through wooded areas fueled his inspiration to create some of the most remarkable resort amenities ever witnessed in Pakistan. Noteworthy among his previous accomplishments are Pinewoods resorts, Pine cottages, Pine villages, and Pine Valley Residency. Now, he dedicates his time and expertise to the promising new venture known as Pine Hills.
Pine Hills, comprised of exquisite residential homes and farmhouses, aims to elevate holiday celebrations and provide residents with a truly exceptional experience. It aspires to create a serene sanctuary where nature seamlessly intertwines with contemporary living, offering an unparalleled lifestyle for its residents. With Major (R) Siddique's vision at its core, Pine Hills sets the stage for an extraordinary residential experience that blends luxury, natural beauty, and a sense of timeless tranquility.
