Park View City

Park View City, an exquisite residential project by Vision Group, holds the prestigious CDA Approval and is located in zone IV of Islamabad. This thoughtfully designed housing society boasts a magnificent 400 ft wide Main Boulevard, offering seamless access directly from the highway into the community. Additionally, entry points via Kurri Road and Banigala provide convenient accessibility.
Renowned for its scenic views and upscale amenities, Park View City has emerged as a sought-after investment opportunity. It caters to families seeking a harmonious living environment in a secure neighborhood with a forward-thinking vision and breathtaking vistas.
This meticulously planned housing venture aims to provide an ideal haven for families, offering a family-friendly atmosphere and a secure abode. With its futuristic approach and captivating views, Park View City sets the stage for a truly extraordinary residential experience.

For a housing society to establish itself as a valid and recognized residential area, it is crucial to undergo registration with the state authorities. In the case of Park View City, it has received a letter number CDA/PLW/Zone-4(94)/12/Vol-I/168, indicating the approval of its NOC (No Objection Certificate) by the Capital Development Authority (CDA). Initially, the NOC was revoked due to challenges related to the access via Malot Road. However, after a successful trial, the NOC was reinstated.
The Park View City Master Plan encompasses a significant portion of the city, outlining the comprehensive layout and development strategy for the society. This master plan serves as a blueprint, guiding the systematic growth and organization of the community.

Park View City Islamabad NOC:

To establish itself as a legitimate and authorized residential area, a housing society must undergo the process of registration with the state authorities. In the case of Park View City, it has obtained the necessary approval from the Capital Development Authority (CDA), as evidenced by the issuance of a NOC (No Objection Certificate) with the letter-number CDA/PLW/Zone-4(94)/12/vol-I/168. Initially, the NOC faced cancellation due to challenges associated with the accessibility through Malot Road. However, following a successful trial period, the NOC was subsequently reinstated, ensuring the society's compliance with the required standards and regulations.

Location of Park View City Islamabad:

When it comes to real estate investment, the location holds paramount importance and is often ranked as the top consideration by professional investors. In this regard, Park View City stands out as a true masterpiece. Situated in a prime location, this housing society offers unparalleled convenience with its proximity to three major highways and the serene Islamabad Zoo and Botanical Gardens in Margallas.
Within the vicinity, several housing societies exist, but few can rival the exceptional location advantages of Park View City Islamabad. Adjacent to Park Enclave and Bahria Enclave, the community enjoys easy access to Bani Gala, ChakShahzad, as well as the Kashmir Highway and Islamabad Highway. Furthermore, the captivating Islamabad Zoo and Botanical Gardens lie just to the north of Park View City, providing a refreshing natural retreat.
Being strategically positioned near prominent city landmarks and yet far enough from the hustle and bustle of noise and pollution, Park View City is an ideal choice for permanent residence. The vast 1200 Kanal land area encompasses diverse residential and commercial blocks, attracting individuals from all walks of life. With its enviable location, Park View City Islamabad offers a unique opportunity for residents to enjoy the best of both convenience and tranquility.

Park View City Master Plan.

Park View City, similar to other prominent housing societies, consists of various residential and commercial blocks. These blocks are further subdivided into distinct residential and commercial sections, each offering plots of varying sizes to cater to different preferences and requirements.

Residential plots of 5 Marla and 10 Marla can be found in Blocks A, B, F, H, J, Overseas, and Golf Estate. Block C offers 10 Marla terrace plots, while Blocks B, E, F, H, J, Golf Estate, and Overseas offer 1 Kanal residential plots. Block D presents the option of 2 Kanal plots. It is important to note that the exact locations of the Overseas Block, Golf Estate, and J Block will be confirmed in the updated master plan of Park View City.
