Kingdom Valley Islamabad NOC:

The No Objection Certificate (NOC) of Kingdom Valley Islamabad is allegedly approved under Naya Pakistan Housing Scheme with registration number “DRG/PHATA/2176-2021”. It is also approved by the Punjab Housing and Town Planning Agency (PHATA). The approved real estate projects have better chances of rapid development.

Kingdom Valley Map and Location:

Kingdom Valley Islamabad is located on the M-2 Islamabad-Lahore motorway, near the Chakri Interchange. Because of the project location, Its an attractive development in the twin cities. Its strategically placed near well-known housing developments in Rawalpindi and Islamabad, such as Mumtaz City, Capital Smart City, Blue World City.
